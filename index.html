What we do

We work on a variety of projects; both big and small. Our job involves designing, cutting and shaping metal. We create new structures and repair existing ones.

We build objects, structures and equipements for construction and manufacturing industries as well as furniture for public and private institutions such as secondary schools, colleges and universities.

Diagrams and sketches

We perform formal diagrams and sketches and construct varied welding parts. We collect parts of components and apply welding practices such as gas-tungsten arc and metal fastening techniques. We render engineering diagrams and welding features as required.

Blueprints

We read and interpret blueprints and engineering plans. We use blueprints and work plans to develop and design welding projects for clients.

Fabrication

Laying out, cutting, fitting, measuring and welding metals to create the desired shape or object is our work. Our team is experienced and knowledgeable in the techniques and tools required to accomplish these tasks.

Inspection

We inspect and test welds and equipment. We check Welds for durability and whether they meet specified standards. We check equipment to determine if repairs are needed or an entirely new equipment is needed.

Professional

We have 10+ years of industry experience in a variety of practices and techniques related to fabrication and welding. They include applying TIG, MIG and ARC, operating metal shop equipment and tools and complying with the industry standards and safety policies.
