About us

Scepter Fabricators is a Fabrication Engineering company. We got started in 2005 and have since then served numerous clients within the Central Rift, North Rift and Western parts of Kenya. Our clients range from universities, technical colleges, polytechnics and schools to construction companies, private entities or businesses and individuals.

We are located at Annex in Eldoret, along Nakuru-Eldoret road.

Our workforce is qualified and experinced in the industry.
