A look at what we do and some of our end products:

Metal fabrication is the process of designing and manufacturing metal structures while following given specifications. The process involves cutting, shaping, bending and assembling (joining of pieces) of metal ot create products. Raw materials used include sheet metal, bar stock, welding rod, metal plate, etc.

To pride the best custom products, we are thorough in following specifications on material thickness, lengh, width, height, weight and metal tolerance.

These are some of our end products which we deliver to our clients:

  • Shelves
  • Tables
  • Seats and chairs
  • Doors
  • Gates
  • Window panes
  • etc
